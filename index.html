Our Vision

Ennovate Lab is an enterprise and innovation hub where creative minds meet to systematically develop and transform communities through technology, creativity and social enterprise. We believe inspired people make the world a better place. These people come from all walks of life but have the unstoppable appetite to solve problems and transform communities. eLab exists to partner, nurture and support such talent because when great minds get together, we all win :)
